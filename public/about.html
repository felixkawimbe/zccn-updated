About Zambia Climate Change Network

Who We Are

Zambia Climate Change Network (ZCCN) is a coalition of civil society organizations working towards climate and environmental justice, sustainable development, and effective climate policy advocacy in Zambia.

Our Mission

To build climate resilience and empower communities through inclusive participation, knowledge sharing, and sustainable practices that align with Zambia‚Äôs national and international commitments on climate change.

Our Vision

A just, sustainable, and climate-resilient Zambia where communities thrive in harmony with the environment.

Our Work

  • Climate change education and awareness campaigns
  • Advocating for stronger climate policies and environmental regulations
  • Supporting community-based climate resilience initiatives
  • Fostering partnerships with local, regional, and global stakeholders
